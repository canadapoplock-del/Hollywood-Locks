Frequently Asked Questions
About Our Locksmith Services

How quickly can you respond to an emergency lockout?

We pride ourselves on fast response times. Our technicians are strategically located throughout Hollywood, FL, and typically arrive within 20-30 minutes of your call. We're available 24/7 for emergency lockouts, including nights, weekends, and holidays.

Do you provide upfront pricing or are there hidden fees?

Absolutely! We believe in complete transparency. We provide upfront quotes over the phone before dispatching a technician. The price we quote is the price you pay - no hidden fees, no surprises. If additional work is needed once on-site, we'll discuss it with you before proceeding.

Will you damage my lock or door when opening it?

Our certified technicians use professional, non-destructive entry techniques whenever possible. We have specialized tools and extensive training to open locks without causing damage. In rare cases where damage is unavoidable, we'll inform you beforehand and can immediately install a replacement lock.

Can you make keys for all types of vehicles, including newer models?

Yes! We have the equipment and expertise to cut and program keys for most vehicle makes and models, including transponder keys, key fobs, and smart keys. We can handle both domestic and foreign vehicles, even the latest models with advanced security systems.

Are your locksmiths licensed and insured?

Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance to protect both our customers and our team. You can trust that you're working with qualified professionals who meet all state and local requirements.

Do you offer any warranty on your lock installation or rekeying services?

Yes, we stand behind our work! All lock installations and rekeying services come with a 90-day warranty on labor. If you experience any issues with our workmanship during this period, we'll return to fix it at no additional charge. Manufacturer warranties on hardware vary by brand.
